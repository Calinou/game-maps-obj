Echo to the Future by dc

This is a derivative of the original map Echo for Cube 2 made by JoJo, W!CK3D and Ulukai, This map is ported to Tesseract with some modifications to geometry and texturing.
There have also been small modifications to accomodate the physics of Red Eclipse 2, with different clipping and obstacle placement.
